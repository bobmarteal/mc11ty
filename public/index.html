Nonprofit consultant, focusing on organizational, leadership and staff development.

Expanding the staff from 1 to 10, building and nurturing a healthy board of directors, creating first-rate educational child-centered programming, growing the development and fundraising functions, as well as the business and community outreach opportunities are key steps in creating an award winning, foundational resource for Ithaca and Tompkins Co.
